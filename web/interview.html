Seeking volunteers for an interview study

In this research project we are trying to understand how Tor users interact with onion services. As part of our project we are looking for Tor users to interview about how they use Tor and onion services. An interview will take 30–60 minutes and we can offer several ways to talk: Signal, WhatsApp, Skype, Jitsi, or phone. To be eligible, you must be 18 years or older and you must have used Tor Browser and onion services (also known as “hidden services”) in the past. We are particularly interested in interviewing non-expert users. To compensate you for your time, we can offer you an Amazon or an Apple gift card worth 20 USD.

To volunteer, please fill out this Google form (we are no longer looking for interviewees).
If selected, we will contact you to schedule a time for the interview.
